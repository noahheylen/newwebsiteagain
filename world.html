All your world are belong to us

UHD Video · Video Game

Cesium for Unreal, used by numerous defense agencies such as the French Air Force, is a 3D geospatial platform that integrates real-world terrain and geospatial data into Unreal Engine with Google Earth Tiles, allowing virtual twin simulations. I simulated the Martian atmosphere in the Atacama desert in Chile, a desert closely resembling the Martian surface, and our largest resource of Lithium.

I also created a drone simulator within the same environment, the drone was only able to fly backwards.

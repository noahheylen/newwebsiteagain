Conflict-Powered

Multimedia installation · 2024

Self-made power banks from throwaway vapes powering two LCD screens.

Throwaway vapes are collected to source their reusable batteries by numerous people and regions around the world. In Ukraine and other conflict zones, these lithium-ion batteries find new life powering small FPV drones, the drones that have transformed warfare over the past few years.
